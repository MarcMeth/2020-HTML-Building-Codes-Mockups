Access to exits

Access to exit means that part of a means of egress within a floor area that provides access to an exit serving the floor area.

Definition overlay example

The width of subsidiary aisles providing access to stored products within an individual storage area may be determined by material handling methods, or other criteria such as minimum width for access to exits or fire protection equipment.
